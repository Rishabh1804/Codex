# Robotics & Autonomous Systems

> Robot control, perception, planning, ROS, autonomous vehicles, and the intersection of AI with physical systems.

### Courses

- [Coursera: Robotics Specialization (Penn)](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/robotics) - Comprehensive robotics fundamentals covering perception, motion planning, and control. `Intermediate`
- [MIT: Autonomous Vehicles (Lex Fridman)](https://selfdrivingcars.mit.edu/) - Deep learning for self-driving cars with lectures and guest talks. `Intermediate`
- [ETH Zurich: Autonomous Mobile Robots](https://ethz.ch/content/dam/ethz/special-interest/mavt/robotics-n-intelligent-systems/asl-dam/documents/lectures/AMR.pdf) - Course materials on autonomous robotics and control. `Advanced`
- [edX: Autonomous Mobile Robots (ETH Zurich)](https://www.edx.org/learn/robotics/eth-zurich-autonomous-mobile-robots) - Free robotics course from a top European institution. `Intermediate`
- [Stanford CS237B: Principles of Robot Autonomy II](https://web.stanford.edu/class/cs237b/) - Advanced robotics covering SLAM, manipulation, and planning. `Advanced`
- [Udacity: Robotics Software Engineer](https://www.udacity.com/course/robotics-software-engineer--nd209) - Free preview content on robotic systems programming. `Intermediate`

### Frameworks & Tools

- [ROS (Robot Operating System) Tutorials](https://wiki.ros.org/ROS/Tutorials) - Official tutorials for the most popular robotics framework. `Beginner`
- [ROS 2 Documentation](https://docs.ros.org/en/humble/) - Modern ROS 2 tutorials and guides. `Beginner`
- [The Construct: ROS Learning](https://www.theconstructsim.com/ros-courses-library/) - Free ROS learning resources with online simulations. `Beginner`
- [Gazebo Simulation Tutorials](https://classic.gazebosim.org/tutorials) - Robot simulation environment tutorials. `Intermediate`
- [MoveIt Tutorials](https://moveit.ros.org/documentation/tutorials/) - Motion planning framework for robotic manipulation. `Intermediate`

### Books & Reading

- [Probabilistic Robotics (Thrun, Burgard, Fox)](http://www.probabilistic-robotics.org/) - Classic robotics textbook with supplementary resources. `Advanced`
- [Modern Robotics (Lynch & Park)](https://hades.mech.northwestern.edu/index.php/Modern_Robotics) - Free textbook with video lectures on robot mechanics and control. `Intermediate`
- [Introduction to Autonomous Robots (Correll et al.)](https://introduction-to-autonomous-robots.github.io/) - Free open textbook on autonomous robotics. `Beginner`
