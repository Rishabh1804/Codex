# AI Art & Image Generation

> Text-to-image models, diffusion techniques, style transfer, creative AI tools, and the art of AI-generated imagery.

### Courses

- [Hugging Face: Diffusion Models Course](https://huggingface.co/learn/diffusion-course/unit0/1) - Hands-on course on building and understanding diffusion models. `Intermediate`
- [DeepLearning.AI: How Diffusion Models Work](https://www.deeplearning.ai/short-courses/how-diffusion-models-work/) - Short course on the math and code behind image generation. `Intermediate`
- [fast.ai: Stable Diffusion from Scratch](https://course.fast.ai/Lessons/part2.html) - Build diffusion models from the ground up. `Advanced`

### Tools & Platforms

- [Stable Diffusion WebUI (AUTOMATIC1111)](https://github.com/AUTOMATIC1111/stable-diffusion-webui) - Popular open-source UI for running Stable Diffusion locally. `Beginner`
- [ComfyUI](https://github.com/comfyanonymous/ComfyUI) - Node-based UI for Stable Diffusion with advanced workflows. `Intermediate`
- [Hugging Face Diffusers](https://huggingface.co/docs/diffusers/index) - Python library for state-of-the-art diffusion models. `Intermediate`
- [InvokeAI](https://invoke-ai.github.io/InvokeAI/) - Open-source creative AI toolkit with professional features. `Beginner`
- [Civitai](https://civitai.com/) - Community platform for sharing models, LoRAs, and prompts. `All Levels`
- [OpenArt](https://openart.ai/) - Platform for discovering AI art workflows and prompts. `Beginner`

### Reading & Research

- [The Illustrated Stable Diffusion (Jay Alammar)](https://jalammar.github.io/illustrated-stable-diffusion/) - Visual explanation of how diffusion models work. `Beginner`
- [Lilian Weng: What are Diffusion Models?](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2021-07-11-diffusion-models/) - In-depth blog post on diffusion model theory. `Intermediate`
- [ControlNet Paper & Code](https://github.com/lllyasviel/ControlNet) - Adding spatial control to diffusion models. `Advanced`
- [Awesome Diffusion Models (GitHub)](https://github.com/diff-usion/Awesome-Diffusion-Models) - Curated list of diffusion model papers and resources. `All Levels`
- [Neural Style Transfer Tutorial (PyTorch)](https://pytorch.org/tutorials/advanced/neural_style_tutorial.html) - Classic style transfer implementation guide. `Intermediate`
