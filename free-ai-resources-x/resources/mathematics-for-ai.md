# Mathematics for AI

> Linear algebra, calculus, probability, statistics, and optimization — the mathematical foundation for all AI.

### Linear Algebra

- [3Blue1Brown: Essence of Linear Algebra](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDPD3MizzM2xVFitgF8hE_ab) - Beautiful visual series that builds deep intuition for linear algebra. `Beginner`
- [MIT 18.06: Linear Algebra (Gilbert Strang)](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-06-linear-algebra-spring-2010/) - The classic MIT linear algebra course — complete with lectures, notes, and exams. `Beginner`
- [Interactive Linear Algebra (GT)](https://textbooks.math.gatech.edu/ila/) - Free interactive online textbook from Georgia Tech. `Beginner`

### Calculus & Optimization

- [3Blue1Brown: Essence of Calculus](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDMsr9K-rj53DwVRMYO3t5Yr) - Visual series that makes calculus intuitive and enjoyable. `Beginner`
- [MIT 18.01/18.02: Single & Multivariable Calculus](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-01sc-single-variable-calculus-fall-2010/) - Complete MIT calculus courses with lectures and problem sets. `Beginner`
- [Convex Optimization (Boyd & Vandenberghe)](https://web.stanford.edu/~boyd/cvxbook/) - Stanford's free textbook on the optimization methods used in ML. `Advanced`

### Probability & Statistics

- [Khan Academy: Statistics & Probability](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/statistics-probability) - Comprehensive, interactive lessons from the ground up. `Beginner`
- [StatQuest (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/c/joshstarmer) - Intuitive, entertaining explanations of statistics and ML math. `Beginner`
- [Harvard: Statistics 110 (Probability)](https://projects.iq.harvard.edu/stat110/home) - Full probability course with video lectures and practice problems. `Intermediate`
- [MIT 18.650: Statistics for Applications](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-650-statistics-for-applications-fall-2016/) - Statistical methods used in data science and ML. `Intermediate`

### Comprehensive Resources

- [Mathematics for Machine Learning (Book)](https://mml-book.github.io/) - Free textbook covering linear algebra, calculus, probability, and optimization for ML. `Intermediate`
- [Coursera: Mathematics for Machine Learning Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/mathematics-machine-learning) - Imperial College London specialization covering linear algebra, multivariate calculus, and PCA. `Beginner`
- [Seeing Theory (Brown University)](https://seeing-theory.brown.edu/) - Interactive visualizations that make probability and statistics intuitive. `Beginner`
- [Matrix Calculus for Deep Learning (Explained.ai)](https://explained.ai/matrix-calculus/) - Free paper explaining matrix calculus specifically for deep learning. `Intermediate`
