# Multimodal AI

> Vision-language models, cross-modal learning, and systems that understand multiple types of data simultaneously.

### Courses

- [CMU: Multimodal Machine Learning](https://cmu-multicomp-lab.github.io/mmml-course/fall2022/) - Comprehensive course on multimodal learning from Carnegie Mellon. `Advanced`
- [Stanford CS231A: Computer Vision with Multi-modal Data](https://web.stanford.edu/class/cs231a/) - Vision course with multimodal components. `Intermediate`
- [DeepLearning.AI: Building Multimodal Search](https://www.deeplearning.ai/short-courses/) - Short course on multimodal search and retrieval. `Intermediate`

### Models & Tools

- [OpenAI CLIP](https://github.com/openai/CLIP) - Vision-language model with documentation and examples. `Intermediate`
- [Hugging Face: Multimodal Models](https://huggingface.co/docs/transformers/main/en/model_doc/auto#multimodal-models) - Guides on using multimodal transformers. `Intermediate`
- [LLaVA (Large Language and Vision Assistant)](https://llava-vl.github.io/) - Open-source multimodal model with training code and demos. `Intermediate`
- [ImageBind (Meta)](https://imagebind.metademolab.com/) - Model that binds six modalities with code and documentation. `Advanced`
- [Stable Diffusion Documentation](https://stability.ai/stable-image) - Text-to-image generation model with extensive community resources. `Intermediate`

### Reading & Research

- [DeepMind: Multimodal Research (Gemini)](https://deepmind.google/technologies/gemini/) - Latest multimodal AI research from Google DeepMind. `All Levels`
- [The Illustrated Stable Diffusion (Jay Alammar)](https://jalammar.github.io/illustrated-stable-diffusion/) - Visual walkthrough of how diffusion models work. `Beginner`
- [arXiv: Multimodal Learning Papers](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.MM/recent) - Latest multimodal AI research papers. `Advanced`
- [Awesome Multimodal ML (GitHub)](https://github.com/pliang279/awesome-multimodal-ml) - Curated reading list of multimodal ML research. `All Levels`
- [LAION: Open Datasets](https://laion.ai/) - Large-scale open datasets for multimodal research. `Intermediate`
