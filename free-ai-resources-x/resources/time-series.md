# Time Series & Forecasting

> Predictive models, sequential data analysis, and techniques for temporal pattern recognition.

### Courses

- [Coursera: Sequences, Time Series and Prediction](https://www.coursera.org/learn/tensorflow-sequences-time-series-and-prediction) - TensorFlow-based time series course by DeepLearning.AI. `Beginner`
- [Kaggle: Time Series Course](https://www.kaggle.com/learn/time-series) - Hands-on course covering trend, seasonality, and forecasting. `Beginner`
- [Penn State: Applied Time Series Analysis](https://online.stat.psu.edu/stat510/) - Comprehensive course on ARIMA, spectral analysis, and more. `Intermediate`
- [Time Series Analysis in Python (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLvcbYUQ5t0UHOLnBzl46_Q6QKtFgfMGc3) - Practical video series covering time series with Python. `Beginner`

### Books & Reading

- [Forecasting: Principles and Practice (Hyndman & Athanasopoulos)](https://otexts.com/fpp3/) - Free comprehensive textbook on modern forecasting methods. `Intermediate`
- [Time Series Forecasting with Python (Notebooks)](https://github.com/marcopeix/TimeSeriesForecastingInPython) - Free notebooks accompanying the time series forecasting book. `Intermediate`
- [Introduction to Time Series and Forecasting (Brockwell & Davis)](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-319-29854-2) - Classic time series textbook (free access through many institutions). `Advanced`

### Tools & Practice

- [Prophet by Meta](https://facebook.github.io/prophet/) - Production-ready forecasting tool with excellent documentation. `Beginner`
- [Nixtla (statsforecast/neuralforecast)](https://nixtla.github.io/statsforecast/) - Open-source time series forecasting libraries. `Intermediate`
- [Darts (Unit8)](https://unit8co.github.io/darts/) - User-friendly time series forecasting library with modern models. `Intermediate`
- [sktime Documentation](https://www.sktime.net/en/stable/) - Unified Python framework for time series ML. `Intermediate`
- [AWS: Time Series Forecasting](https://aws.amazon.com/forecast/getting-started/) - Free tutorials on time series ML with AWS. `Intermediate`
- [tslearn Documentation](https://tslearn.readthedocs.io/) - Machine learning tools for time series data. `Intermediate`
