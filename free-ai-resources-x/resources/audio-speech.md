# Audio & Speech Processing

> Speech recognition, text-to-speech, audio classification, music generation, and audio ML.

### Courses

- [Hugging Face: Audio Course](https://huggingface.co/learn/audio-course/chapter0/introduction) - Comprehensive course on audio processing with transformers. `Beginner`
- [Coursera: Audio Signal Processing for Music Applications](https://www.coursera.org/learn/audio-signal-processing) - Stanford/UPF course on audio analysis fundamentals. `Intermediate`
- [Python for Audio Signal Processing (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL-wATfeyAMNqIee7cH3q1bh4QJFAaeNv0) - Practical video series on audio ML with Python. `Beginner`
- [MIT: Music Technology](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/21m-380-music-and-technology-contemporary-history-and-aesthetics-fall-2009/) - Free MIT course on the intersection of music and technology. `Beginner`

### Tools & Libraries

- [SpeechBrain](https://speechbrain.github.io/) - Open-source PyTorch toolkit for speech and audio processing. `Intermediate`
- [Mozilla Common Voice](https://commonvoice.mozilla.org/en/datasets) - Free open speech dataset for building voice applications. `All Levels`
- [AssemblyAI Tutorials](https://www.assemblyai.com/blog) - Practical guides on speech-to-text and audio AI. `Beginner`
- [Whisper (OpenAI)](https://github.com/openai/whisper) - Open-source speech recognition model with documentation and examples. `Intermediate`
- [Librosa Documentation](https://librosa.org/doc/latest/index.html) - Python library for audio and music analysis with tutorials. `Beginner`
- [ESPnet](https://github.com/espnet/espnet) - End-to-end speech processing toolkit with recipes and tutorials. `Advanced`

### Reading & Research

- [Music Information Retrieval (MIR) Tutorials](https://musicinformationretrieval.com/) - Free interactive notebooks on audio features and analysis. `Intermediate`
- [Kaldi Documentation](https://kaldi-asr.org/doc/) - Toolkit for speech recognition research with extensive guides. `Advanced`
- [Awesome Speech Recognition (GitHub)](https://github.com/zzw922cn/awesome-speech-recognition-speech-synthesis-papers) - Curated list of speech recognition and synthesis papers. `All Levels`
