# Natural Language Processing (NLP)

> Text analysis, language models, transformers, and techniques for teaching machines to understand human language.

### Courses

- [Stanford CS224N: NLP with Deep Learning](https://web.stanford.edu/class/cs224n/) - Premier NLP course with lecture videos, notes, and assignments. `Intermediate`
- [Hugging Face: NLP Course](https://huggingface.co/learn/nlp-course/chapter1/1) - Hands-on course on transformers, fine-tuning, and modern NLP. `Beginner`
- [Coursera: NLP Specialization (DeepLearning.AI)](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/natural-language-processing) - 4-course specialization from classification to attention models. `Intermediate`
- [spaCy: Advanced NLP Course](https://course.spacy.io/en/) - Free interactive course on production-ready NLP with spaCy. `Beginner`
- [CMU CS11-747: Neural Networks for NLP](https://www.phontron.com/class/nn4nlp2024/) - Advanced NLP course by Graham Neubig with videos and assignments. `Advanced`
- [fast.ai: NLP with Code-First Approach](https://www.fast.ai/posts/2019-07-08-fastai-nlp.html) - Practical NLP course focused on coding from the start. `Intermediate`

### Books & Reading

- [Speech and Language Processing (Jurafsky & Martin)](https://web.stanford.edu/~jurafsky/slp3/) - The comprehensive NLP textbook, freely available online. `Intermediate`
- [Natural Language Processing with Transformers (Notebooks)](https://github.com/nlp-with-transformers/notebooks) - Free Jupyter notebooks from the O'Reilly book. `Intermediate`
- [The Illustrated Transformer (Jay Alammar)](https://jalammar.github.io/illustrated-transformer/) - Visual, intuitive explanation of the Transformer architecture. `Beginner`
- [Lilian Weng's Blog](https://lilianweng.github.io/) - Exceptional blog posts explaining NLP and ML concepts in depth. `Intermediate`

### Tools & Practice

- [Hugging Face Model Hub](https://huggingface.co/models) - Thousands of pre-trained NLP models ready to use. `All Levels`
- [NLTK Documentation](https://www.nltk.org/) - Classic Python NLP library with tutorials and corpora. `Beginner`
- [Kaggle: NLP Competitions](https://www.kaggle.com/competitions?tags=13204-NLP) - Practice NLP skills on real-world competition datasets. `All Levels`
- [LangChain Documentation](https://python.langchain.com/docs/get_started/introduction) - Build applications with LLMs — great for learning modern NLP pipelines. `Intermediate`
