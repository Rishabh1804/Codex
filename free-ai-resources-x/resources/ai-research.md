# AI Research & Papers

> Staying current with AI research — finding, reading, and understanding cutting-edge papers and publications.

### Paper Repositories

- [arXiv: AI & ML Papers](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/recent) - The primary repository of AI research papers, updated daily. `All Levels`
- [Papers With Code](https://paperswithcode.com/) - Research papers linked with code implementations and benchmarks. `Intermediate`
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/) - AI-powered research discovery tool with paper recommendations. `All Levels`
- [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/) - Comprehensive academic search engine for finding papers. `All Levels`
- [Connected Papers](https://www.connectedpapers.com/) - Visual tool to explore academic paper connections and related work. `All Levels`

### Research Blogs & Summaries

- [Distill.pub](https://distill.pub/) - Interactive, beautifully visualized explanations of ML research. `Intermediate`
- [Two Minute Papers (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/c/K%C3%A1rolyZsolnai) - Engaging video summaries of cutting-edge AI research. `Beginner`
- [The Batch (DeepLearning.AI)](https://www.deeplearning.ai/the-batch/) - Andrew Ng's weekly AI news and research digest. `All Levels`
- [Google Research Blog](https://research.google/blog/) - Latest AI research from Google. `Intermediate`
- [OpenAI Research](https://openai.com/research) - Research publications from OpenAI. `Intermediate`
- [Anthropic Research](https://www.anthropic.com/research) - AI safety and capabilities research from Anthropic. `Intermediate`

### How to Read Papers

- [How to Read a Paper (Keshav)](http://ccr.sigcomm.org/online/back/2007/jul/p83-keshavA.pdf) - Classic guide on efficiently reading research papers. `Beginner`
- [Yannic Kilcher (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/@YannicKilcher) - Detailed paper walkthroughs and explanations. `Intermediate`
- [ML Paper Club Resources](https://github.com/dair-ai/ml-papers-of-the-week) - Weekly curated ML papers worth reading. `All Levels`
