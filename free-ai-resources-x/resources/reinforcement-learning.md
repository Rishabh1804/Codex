# Reinforcement Learning

> Algorithms that learn through interaction with environments — from game AI to robotics control.

### Courses

- [David Silver's RL Course (DeepMind/UCL)](https://www.davidsilver.uk/teaching/) - The classic RL course by the co-creator of AlphaGo, with videos and slides. `Intermediate`
- [DeepMind x UCL: RL Lecture Series](https://www.deepmind.com/learning-resources/reinforcement-learning-lecture-series-2021) - Comprehensive, modern RL course from DeepMind researchers. `Intermediate`
- [Coursera: Reinforcement Learning Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/reinforcement-learning) - University of Alberta specialization covering fundamentals to advanced algorithms. `Intermediate`
- [Stanford CS234: Reinforcement Learning](https://web.stanford.edu/class/cs234/) - Stanford's RL course with lecture videos, notes, and assignments. `Intermediate`
- [Hugging Face: Deep RL Course](https://huggingface.co/learn/deep-rl-course/unit0/introduction) - Hands-on course with interactive notebooks and model sharing. `Beginner`
- [UC Berkeley CS285: Deep RL](https://rail.eecs.berkeley.edu/deeprlcourse/) - Sergey Levine's deep RL course with full lecture videos. `Advanced`

### Books & Reading

- [Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction (Sutton & Barto)](http://incompleteideas.net/book/the-book-2nd.html) - The definitive RL textbook, freely available online. `Intermediate`
- [OpenAI Spinning Up in Deep RL](https://spinningup.openai.com/) - Excellent educational resource with theory, code, and exercises. `Intermediate`
- [Algorithms for Decision Making (MIT)](https://algorithmsbook.com/) - Free textbook covering decision-making under uncertainty. `Advanced`

### Practice & Tools

- [OpenAI Gymnasium](https://gymnasium.farama.org/) - Standard toolkit for developing and comparing RL algorithms. `All Levels`
- [Stable-Baselines3 Documentation](https://stable-baselines3.readthedocs.io/) - Reliable RL algorithm implementations with great tutorials. `Intermediate`
- [CleanRL](https://docs.cleanrl.dev/) - Single-file RL implementations that are easy to understand and modify. `Intermediate`
- [PettingZoo](https://pettingzoo.farama.org/) - Multi-agent RL environment library. `Advanced`
