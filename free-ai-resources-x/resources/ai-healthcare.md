# AI in Healthcare

> Medical imaging, drug discovery, clinical decision support, and AI applications transforming medicine.

### Courses

- [Stanford: AI in Healthcare Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/ai-healthcare) - Comprehensive course covering clinical data, medical imaging, and AI applications. `Intermediate`
- [MIT: Machine Learning for Healthcare](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-s897-machine-learning-for-healthcare-spring-2019/) - MIT OpenCourseWare on ML in medicine with lectures and assignments. `Advanced`
- [Coursera: AI for Medical Diagnosis](https://www.coursera.org/learn/ai-for-medical-diagnosis) - DeepLearning.AI course on diagnostic AI systems. `Intermediate`
- [Coursera: AI for Medical Prognosis](https://www.coursera.org/learn/ai-for-medical-prognosis) - Building prognostic models for patient outcomes. `Intermediate`
- [Google Health: AI in Medicine](https://health.google/health-research/) - Research publications and resources on healthcare AI. `All Levels`

### Books & Reading

- [Deep Medicine (Resources)](https://drerictopol.com/portfolio/deep-medicine/) - Resources and talks on AI transforming healthcare. `Beginner`
- [MIT Critical Data: Secondary Analysis of EHR](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262543217/secondary-analysis-of-electronic-health-records/) - Free book on analyzing electronic health records. `Advanced`
- [DeepMind Health Research (AlphaFold)](https://www.deepmind.com/research/highlighted-research/alphafold) - Cutting-edge AI research for protein structure prediction. `Advanced`

### Datasets & Practice

- [Kaggle: Healthcare Datasets](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets?tags=13107-Health) - Free real-world healthcare datasets for practice. `All Levels`
- [MIMIC-III Clinical Database](https://mimic.mit.edu/) - Free critical care database for health-related ML research. `Advanced`
- [NIH Chest X-ray Dataset](https://nihcc.app.box.com/v/ChestXray-NIHCC) - Large-scale chest X-ray dataset for medical imaging research. `Intermediate`
- [Grand Challenge](https://grand-challenge.org/) - Platform with biomedical image analysis challenges and datasets. `Intermediate`
- [fast.ai: Medical Imaging](https://www.fast.ai/) - Practical deep learning applied to medical imaging examples. `Intermediate`
