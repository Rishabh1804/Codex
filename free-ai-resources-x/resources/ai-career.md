# AI Career & Interview Prep

> Career guidance, interview preparation, portfolio building, and navigating the AI job market.

### Interview Preparation

- [Chip Huyen: ML Interviews Book](https://huyenchip.com/ml-interviews-book/) - Comprehensive free guide to ML interview preparation. `Intermediate`
- [ML Interview Questions Repository](https://github.com/andrewekhalel/MLQuestions) - Curated collection of ML interview questions and answers. `Intermediate`
- [Deep Learning Interviews Book](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.00650) - Free book of deep learning interview questions. `Intermediate`
- [ML System Design (GitHub)](https://github.com/chiphuyen/machine-learning-systems-design) - Guide to ML system design interviews. `Advanced`

### Career Development

- [DeepLearning.AI: AI For Everyone](https://www.coursera.org/learn/ai-for-everyone) - Understanding AI career paths and business opportunities. `Beginner`
- [Kaggle: Getting Started](https://www.kaggle.com/learn) - Build portfolio projects and earn certificates. `Beginner`
- [fast.ai: Practical Deep Learning](https://course.fast.ai/) - Build impressive portfolio projects with practical skills. `Beginner`
- [Google: Machine Learning Career Resources](https://ai.google/education/) - Career development resources from Google. `All Levels`
- [Microsoft: AI Skills Challenge](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/topics/ai) - Free learning paths with certificates. `All Levels`

### Portfolio & Community

- [GitHub Student Developer Pack](https://education.github.com/pack) - Free tools for students building AI portfolios. `Beginner`
- [Hugging Face: Spaces](https://huggingface.co/spaces) - Deploy ML demos for free as portfolio pieces. `Beginner`
- [Kaggle Competitions](https://www.kaggle.com/competitions) - Build competitive ML experience and ranking. `All Levels`
- [Papers With Code: Benchmarks](https://paperswithcode.com/sota) - Understand state-of-the-art to prep for research roles. `Intermediate`
- [AI/ML Communities (Discord, Reddit)](https://www.reddit.com/r/MachineLearning/) - Connect with practitioners and stay current. `All Levels`
