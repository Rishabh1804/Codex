# AI Ethics

> Responsible AI, bias detection, fairness, safety, alignment, and the societal impact of artificial intelligence.

### Courses

- [Fast.ai: Practical Data Ethics](https://ethics.fast.ai/) - Hands-on course on ethical implications of data science and AI. `Beginner`
- [MIT: Ethics of AI](https://mit-serc.pubpub.org/ai-ethics) - Exploring ethical considerations in AI development and deployment. `Beginner`
- [Elements of AI: Ethics Module](https://www.elementsofai.com/) - AI ethics within a comprehensive, beginner-friendly AI course. `Beginner`
- [Coursera: AI Ethics in Practice](https://www.coursera.org/learn/ai-ethics) - Free to audit course on applying ethical principles to AI. `Beginner`
- [Harvard: Ethics and Governance of AI](https://pll.harvard.edu/course/ethics-and-governance-artificial-intelligence) - Harvard's perspective on AI governance and ethics. `Intermediate`

### Books & Reading

- [Fairness and Machine Learning (Barocas, Hardt, Narayanan)](https://fairmlbook.org/) - Free textbook on fairness, bias, and accountability in ML. `Intermediate`
- [AI Ethics Brief (Montreal AI Ethics Institute)](https://montrealethics.ai/) - Weekly digests and research on AI ethics. `All Levels`
- [Anthropic: AI Safety Research](https://www.anthropic.com/research) - Free research papers on AI alignment and safety. `Advanced`
- [The Alignment Problem (Resources)](https://brianchristian.org/the-alignment-problem/) - Resources from the book on AI alignment challenges. `Beginner`

### Organizations & Guides

- [Google: Responsible AI Practices](https://ai.google/responsibilities/responsible-ai-practices/) - Guide on building AI responsibly from Google. `All Levels`
- [Stanford HAI: AI Policy Resources](https://hai.stanford.edu/policy-resources) - Research and resources on AI ethics and policy. `Intermediate`
- [Partnership on AI](https://partnershiponai.org/resources/) - Resources, case studies, and guidelines for responsible AI. `All Levels`
- [AI Fairness 360 (IBM)](https://aif360.mybluemix.net/) - Open-source toolkit to detect and mitigate bias in ML models. `Intermediate`
- [Model Cards for Model Reporting](https://modelcards.withgoogle.com/about) - Framework for transparent model documentation. `Beginner`
